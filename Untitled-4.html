About Me

I am Manoj Godar, an Electrical Engineer specializing in the integration of renewable energy resources and distributed energy resources into power grids. With a robust foundation in electrical engineering, I have developed extensive skills in simulation software such as MATLAB and Proteus, which I have applied to various projects focused on enhancing grid stability and modernization.

My primary research interests lie in grid stability and the seamless integration of renewable energy sources into existing power systems. I am particularly interested in advanced control mechanisms for voltage regulation, dynamic line rating technologies, and innovative grid modernization techniques.

I am currently seeking a PhD position in Power System Engineering, where I aim to delve deeper into grid stability and renewable energy integration. My goal is to contribute to the development of resilient, efficient, and sustainable power systems that can support the growing demand for renewable energy.
